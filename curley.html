Recipe for fruit (I prefer strawberry) crumble

With times like these, the prices of butter, flour, sugar have gone up. So my ma devised this good recipe. The wife makes this weekly at this point.

1. Grab some strawberries (only the red part), sugar, cornstarch, water, and some vanilla beans if you are a rich person, and whatever else you got that can sweeten something up like raisins, or not I do not care at all.

2. Mix those ingredients with each other so now you have yourselves something filling.

3. Grab some oats, flour, sugar, molasses (or sugar with molasses infused, the new people call it brown sugar), baking powder, some cinnamon, a pinch of salt, and as much melted butter as possible (or just enough to moisten everything else)

4. Throw it in the oven until the top is bubbling and the smell is everywhere on your ranch.

5. Grab some of the local ice cream vendor's ice cream and put it on the crumble while it is bubbling.

6. Enjoy


Probably the best recipe this side of California.

Curley (May 1931)
