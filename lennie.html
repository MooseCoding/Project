George

George, you won't ever believe this! I was told to write to you from the guy. Said that I was to write you bout everyting I have now.

The nice old man said that you are comin here shortly. I can't wait to take you to the rabits. They are amazin'. So fluffy, big, calm.

That stable buck also is here, came here bout 5-10 years 'go. Said the same thing as that Candy. Im just so excited to see you again

Lennie Smalls, May 1949
