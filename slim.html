What it takes to be a good manager:

5 things to do to become the best manager on a ranch:

      1. Be strong: You can show weakness but don't allow anyone to take advantage of them
      2. Have good morals: Don't shoot a dog for no reason, shoot it if it is in pain and going to die
      3. Defend your workers: Someone punches a worker, you fight them
      4. Be kind: Without being kind you turn into the enemy, you dont want to be the enemy
      5. Expect what you could give: Don't expect someone to do something you wouldn'y do

That is all for now

The best man in the West,
Slim (July 1931)
